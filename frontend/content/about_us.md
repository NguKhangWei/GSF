# About Us Page

## Main Description 
- GolfSmart Group, which started out as GolfSmart Fitting Studio, was founded by Mr Jack Loi, who is a Malaysian golf enthusiast with certification by USGTF as a certified teaching professional and also a certified club fitter by International Clubmakers Guild (ICG) more than 11 years ago when he feels that there is a gap in the industry to offer advanced fitting and a selection of shafts to golfers that demands nothing less than the best. Soon after, recognising the important of scientific and modern golf coaching to enhance the sport, he founded Swing Dynamic Academy with a stable of qualified and experienced teaching professionals from USGTF and Tour players.
Since its inception, GolfSmart Group has established and well recognized in the market as a professional golf corporate with its pool of professionally trained club fitters and professional golf teachers. GolfSmart has since been recognised by golf equipment principals like the renowned brands of Oban , Aerotech and Kaen shaft as well as Kuro and PXG to represent the brand as their authorized dealer, distributor and club fitting studio. GoflSmart group has since expanded into other parts of the world with strategic partner in Asia, Europe and USA.

- GolfSmart Fitting Studio has a mission to deliver the extreme performance clubs for golf fanatics Worldwide who are seeking  for improved and excellent performance to their game and assuring its customers a full satisfaction with its high tech fitting facilities and knowledge in club fitting requirements.
